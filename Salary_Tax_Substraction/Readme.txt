---Salary Tax Calculator (Python)---

This simple Python program helps you calculate your net salary after applying tax based on whether you are a Filer or Non-Filer.

---How to Use

Run the program in any Python environment (IDLE, VS Code, or Terminal).

When prompted, enter your salary (numeric value).

Then, type whether you are a Filer or Non-Filer.

Example inputs:

filer

nonfiler

The program will automatically:

Apply 15% tax if you are a Filer

Apply 20% tax if you are a Non-Filer

Finally, it will display your net salary after tax.

---Example Output
Enter your salary: 100000
Enter your status (Filer/Non-Filer): Filer
Your net salary after tax is: 85000.0

---Features

Handles case sensitivity (e.g., Filer, filer, FiLeR all work)

Automatically removes extra spaces or dashes in input

Simple and beginner-friendly logic

Perfect for Python practice and tax concept understanding

---Requirements

Python 3.6 or above

No external libraries required
